Requests vs. Implementation

WHAT WAS REQUESTED WHAT WE DELIVERED

Better Food Tracking

You wanted an easier way to log meals exactly as you eat them, including flexible portion sizes.

Detailed Daily Planner

We added a clear daily overview showing exactly what you need to eat. Logging a meal now lets you adjust the exact amount you ate, and anyone can easily tweak their goals without feeling restricted.

Instant Plan Updates

You asked for a quick way to get a new meal plan if your current one isn't working out today.

One-Click Refresh

We added a simple "Refresh" button. With one click, your dashboard instantly updates with a brand new, personalized meal plan for the day.

Smarter AI Coach Conversations

Chatting with the AI coach felt a bit clunky, especially when writing longer messages or getting recipe advice.

Natural and Clean Chatting

You can now easily write longer messages with paragraph breaks. We've also updated the Nutrition AI to guide you on how to ask better questions (for example, asking for "150g chicken pan-fried in 1 tbsp oil" instead of just "chicken"), which helps the AI give you much more accurate and helpful advice.

Smoother App Experience

You noticed some visual glitches, screen freezing, and hard-to-read text in certain modes.

Bug Fixes & Polish

We fixed delays in the workout rest timer, made the text much easier to read when using Light Mode, and solved a hidden error that caused occasional app crashes.

New Client Accounts & Roster Seeding

The coach wanted four specific clients (Rozain, Nuqta, Provit, Numa) in the system assigned directly to their roster.

Automated Client & Plan Onboarding

Created accounts for Rozain, Nuqta, Provit, and Numa, computed their health profiles, generated tailored workout/nutrition plans, and assigned them to Coach Noroze Sikandar. Note: Clients can re-take the onboarding questionnaire at any time to re-customize their profiles and plans according to their own needs.
